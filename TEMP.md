## Matching
To match the shows you need a dir like this
```bash
/Media
├── SHOW
│   ├── Season 1
│   │   └── Episode 1.mp4
│   │   └── Episode 2.mp4
│   │   └── ...
│   └── Season 2
│       └── Episode 1.mp4
│       └── Episode 2.mp4
│       └── ...
└── ...
```
